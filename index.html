Peru

Peru is a country located in South America. It is bordered in the north by Ecuador and Colombia, in the east by Brazil, in the southeast by Bolivia, in the south by Chile, and in the south and west by the Pacific Ocean. Peru's mains economical activities are exports and tourism. We have the beatiful ruins of Macchu Picchu, which is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.Lima city is the capital of the country and also the culinary capital for the country, where is located Central restaurant categorized as the best resturant in the world.
